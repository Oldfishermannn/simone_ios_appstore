Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Overview

Simone is an AI music companion that generates real-time instrumental music. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

  • No account or login required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No personal information stored on our servers

How the App Works

  • The app connects directly to Google's Generative AI API to generate music in real-time
  • Only music generation parameters (style, mood, playback controls) are transmitted
  • Audio is streamed back to your device and played locally
  • No audio is recorded or stored
  • No intermediary servers — your device communicates directly with Google's API

Local Storage

The app stores your preferences (such as pinned music styles) locally on your device using standard system storage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Music is generated using Google's Lyria AI model. The app connects directly to Google's Generative AI API. No personal data is shared with Google or any third party.
